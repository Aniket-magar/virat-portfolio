Key highlights
01
100* off 74 balls vs West Indies — 10 fours & 4 sixes. Called the "innings of the tournament" by commentators and earned Man of the Match.
02
One of only three batsmen in the entire tournament to score a century; finished as the 3rd highest run-scorer overall.
03
Semi-final all-rounder: 43 runs + 2/27 vs New Zealand — second consecutive Player of the Match award.
04
Led India to a 12-run D/L victory in the final vs South Africa on 2 March 2008 in Kuala Lumpur — India's second U19 World Cup title.
05
Team also featured Ravindra Jadeja (vice-captain), Pradeep Sangwan, and Tanmay Srivastava — several of whom went on to senior international careers.
