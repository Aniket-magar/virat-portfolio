Defining Moments

Moments That Defined Their Era

2015
AB's 133* off 59 Balls
Against Mumbai Indians, AB de Villiers played one of the greatest innings in IPL history. Virat watched from the other end and the dressing room in pure awe. 12 sixes. Zero mercy.
All-time IPL Classic
2016
Virat's 973 Runs — World Record
The single greatest batting season in IPL history. 4 centuries, 7 fifties. AB scored 687 alongside him. Together they carried RCB to the final in a season the world will never forget.
World Record Season
2016
229* Unbroken vs Gujarat Lions
Virat 109*, AB 129*. An unbroken partnership that stunned Gujarat Lions. Two friends at their peak, in the same team, feeding off each other's energy. The crowd never sat down.
Greatest RCB Partnership
2018
AB Stays Loyal After Retirement
When AB retired from international cricket, he chose to keep playing IPL — specifically for RCB, specifically because of Virat Kohli. Loyalty, not just cricket, kept him in red.
Brotherhood Over Everything
2020
UAE Playoffs Together
In an empty-stadium COVID edition, AB and Virat guided RCB to the playoffs with match-winning knocks. Proving their magic worked even without a roaring Chinnaswamy behind them.
UAE Edition
2021
AB's Final Season & Retirement
AB de Villiers played his final IPL season for RCB in 2021, then retired from all cricket in November. Virat's emotional farewell for his closest friend in cricket moved fans across the world.
End of an Era
Playing with Virat at RCB was one of the greatest joys of my career. Some partnerships feel like home — ours was exactly that. I stayed in the IPL because of him, because of what we built together at RCB.
AB de Villiers — on his decision to stay at RCB after international retirement
