The Full Era

They Carried India
On Their Shoulders

For nearly two decades, when India needed runs the world looked at these two. No excuses. No off days. Just show up and deliver for 1.4 billion people.

From Edgbaston to Adelaide, from Eden Gardens to Wankhede — whenever the pressure was at maximum, Rohit and Virat were at the crease. Together, they were India's answer to every crisis.

They didn't just play cricket — they carried the hopes, prayers, and dreams of a nation for nearly two decades. And they did it with grace, fire, and unflinching love for the blue jersey.

End of an Era

They Walked Away Together

Not every story has a final chapter — some become the whole book. These two wrote India's greatest cricket era together, and chose to close it the same way they lived it: side by side.
2024T20 International Retirement
After lifting the T20 World Cup in the West Indies, both Rohit Sharma and Virat Kohli announced their retirement from T20Is on the same night — June 29, 2024. Together they bowed out of the format that made them global icons.
2025Test Cricket Farewell
Virat Kohli announced his Test retirement in May 2025. Rohit Sharma stepped back from Tests around the same period. The two pillars of Indian red-ball cricket closed a chapter forever — within weeks of each other.
Their jerseys. Their era. Our memories. Forever.
